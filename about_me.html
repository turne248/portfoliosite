Who Am I?

Greetings, you've stumbled across my portfolio website. My name is Robert Turner, but I'll also go by Robby, Bob, Bert, etc. I'm not picky. Currently, I am a Master's student at IUPUI, with my focus in Motion Design and User Interface Design.

As my portfolio suggests, when it comes to Computer Graphics as a whole, I'm fascinated by the many different paths of creativity that exist, whether it be Graphic Design, Motion Graphics, Web Design, etc.

Currently, I am passionate about creating animations and graphics that are eye-catching and useful for promotion and self-identification. My plan is to approach the world of creativity and aesthetics with an analytical, logical mindset, as behind every great design, there is a rationale to how it came to be. In addition, I use my gifts of communication, translating them to communicating visually through still and motion graphics, not just written word on the page. In a company, I value being a part of a close-knit group working collaboratively to generate the next great idea.
