Hi! My name is Robby Turner and I'm a designer currently living in Indianapolis. In 2022 I graduated from IU Indianapolis, receiving my Master's in Media Arts and Sciences and certification in Human Computer Interaction.

Since the beginning of my professional journey, I prided myself on bringing a multi-disciplanary approach and I've had the opportunity to dabble in a variety of spaces under the design umbrella. I value close-knit collaboration in the ideation process, out of which sparks creativity and drives thoughtful solutions. I view a designer's role as one of a storyteller â€” weaving meaning and emotion into every project.

I believe in carrying strong values with me as a creative and a professional. I believe the ideation process isn't truly meaningful if the space isn't inclusive. I also believe designers have a responsibility to consider ethics on equal, if not greater, footing to function and form. Projects carry impact whether intended or not, so it's crucial to evaluate said impact and involve any and all stakeholders in the design process.

My industry experience has primed me for future opportunities, be they in websites, graphics, video, or frontiers new to me. If you're interested in me or my work, I'd love to connect with you.
